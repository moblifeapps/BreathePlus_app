Match-3
Toss all of the balls onto the board to fill up all of the spaces. Think ahead to make sure the entire board gets filled. Tap on the red balls to move them onto the board, if a space is filled the balls will jump over and fill the next space. Try to complete all 50 levels in this fun brain teaser online puzzle game.

Triple Jack is a version of Black Jack where you can make three stacks on the playing field. The combo meter rises every time you get 21. Go over 21 and you are bust. Bust three times and it's game over.

Penguin Adventure is a puzzle game where you need to control the penguin to collect all diamonds and to unfreeze his girlfriend.There are nine levels in this game and every level is much harder than before. Try to pass all levels and to enjoy this puzzle game. You can continue from last time if you play this game again.
